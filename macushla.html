Macushla

Who

Stefan Andre Waligur - Musician/Composer

Time-frame

August 2015

Role

Design and Develop

Client Goal

The client wanted to update his website, which was hosted on a drag and drop website. His goal was to appeal to a younger generation, and have his new website showcase his work and direct the user towards purchasing his albums, and clearly lay out his tour dates.

Solution

I designed and updated the website on the existing medium and hosting site, adding custom HTML elements. To modernize the site, I used large high-quality images and prominently incorporated the links to social media and his blog. I put links to his newest album on the front page, as well as created a store page with easy links to the most popular websites where young people buy their music.
