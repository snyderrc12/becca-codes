Who

For Snyder & Company

Time-frame

October 2015

Role

Design and Code

Goal

The client wanted to have an email format that would be easier to read and more engaging than the Constant Contact version used previously. He also wanted it to coordinate with his brand. In addition, a main goal was to place attention on deadlines, and to a call to action to visit his website or contact the office.

Solution

I designed the email using HTML and inline-CSS. I used the same color palette that is used on his current website to coordinate between them. For a call to action, I used color to pop out a button to visit the website, just below clear contact information.
